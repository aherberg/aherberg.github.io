Da Tor Mistet Hammeren Sin

A cartoon about Thor (in Norwegian).

  • Role: Illustrator / Producer / Voice Actor / Composer
  • Time: Mar 2011
  • Project Type: School Project

I took an assignment from my Norwegian 103 class way beyond its requirements. I always wanted to produce a cartoon and thought this would be a great opportunity since the script was already written!

// Hero Image //

The script of the story is based on an old Norse Mythology story where Thor lost his hammer. I took this story and reimagined it as if it were an episode of "Adventure Time", and used that illustrative style to inspire my drawings and characters.

// IMAGES? //

To bring my drawings to life, I scanned them into Photoshop, added color, and then created a series of images where I super-imposed various mouth-shapes.

// ILLUSTRATIONS //

I then took the images and did a stop-animation style movie in iMovie to come up with the final cartoon. I also wrote and recorded a fun theme song to uphold the feel of Adventure Time in the final product.

  • Teammates: Chrissy Hettich, Jacob Monson, Linnea Bickeboeller
  • Tools: Photoshop, iMovie, Garageband, Pencil, Paper
  • Class: Norwegian 103, Spring 2012
